### Key Learning: ESC9 UPN Manipulation

**Why revert UPN?** The certificate is issued with the spoofed UPN (Administrator), but authentication fails if the account still has that UPN in Active Directory. The KDC checks if the UPN matches a valid account. By reverting:
- Certificate retains "Administrator" UPN
- ca_operator's actual account goes back to normal
- KDC validates the certificate against the spoofed identity, not the real account

**Critical insight:** The certificate is a "snapshot" of the UPN at request time. The account can be reverted immediately after requesting the certificate.
